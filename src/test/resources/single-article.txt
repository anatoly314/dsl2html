	[m4][b]restriction, constraint, restraint or limitation?[/b]
	[m4]{{para}}These are all things that limit what you can do. A {{/para}}[b]{{arbd1}}restriction{{/arbd1}} [/b]{{para}} is rule or law that is made by sb in authority. A {{/para}}[b]{{arbd1}}constraint{{/arbd1}} [/b]{{para}} is sth that exists rather than sth that is made, although it may exist as a result of sb's decision. A {{/para}}[b]{{arbd1}}restraint{{/arbd1}} [/b]{{para}} is also sth that exists: it can exist outside yourself, as the result of sb else's decision; but it can also exist inside you, as a fear of what other people may think or as your own feeling about what is acceptable: {{/para}}[ex][*][b]▪[/b] {{unx}}moral/social/cultural restraints.{{/unx}} [/*][/ex]{{para}} A {{/para}}[b]{{arbd1}}limitation{{/arbd1}} [/b]{{para}} is more general and can be a rule that sb makes or a fact or condition that exists.{{/para}}
